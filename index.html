About Us

We are a Multi-College Tech Community that helps college students to learn in public and engage with similar-minded people.

We help local college communities to encourage this culture on their campus and help them to build it.

We help upskill in the domain of your interests by organizing workshops, technical seminars, hackathons, project sessions, code-along, etc.

The Community aims to help individuals to Collaborate and Contribute to Open-Source Projects by mentoring them at each stage of their Contribution.

Our Vision

To Support Individuals working together to create communities of practice in which the healthy Open-Source ecosystem thrives.

Our Mission

  • To FURNISH Industrial environment and experience.
  • To EDUCATE people about Open-Source Opportunities.
  • To INSTITUTE a Cohesive and interactive Community.
  • Overall Growth and Development of individual Personality.

What you will gain from us?

Getting started with your lifelong journey of coding.

Live training sessions regarding programming, open source etc.

Improve your Interpersonal skills.

Explore and Learn what real-world Software development looks like.

Refine your Problem-Solving skills.

Collaborative journey of learning, practising and sharing technical skills with our enthusiastic community!!
